StudyForge is a **free, AI-powered study app** designed to help students turn notes, PDFs, and images into interactive **flashcards, quizzes, and tests**. Think of it as a smarter, fully AI-driven version of Quizlet, completely free and fully in-browser‚Äîno accounts or paywalls required.  

---

## Features üöÄ

- **AI-Powered Flashcards & Quizzes**  
  Automatically generate flashcards and quizzes from your notes, PDFs, or images (PNG, JPEG). Covers **all content** provided, not just a limited set.  

- **Multiple Study Modes**  
  - **Learn Mode**: Gradually introduces flashcards, repeating missed ones more frequently.  
  - **Test Mode**: Simulates exams with no instant feedback.  
  - **Review Mode**: Focuses on flashcards that were previously missed.  

- **Spaced Repetition**  
  Automatically resurface flashcards based on your performance and study history to maximize long-term retention.  

- **Edit & Customize AI Output**  
  Modify, add, delete, or merge flashcards, or use AI to simplify, make harder, or add examples to any card.  

- **Auto-Generated Study Guides**  
  Get AI summaries, key takeaways, and likely exam questions from your notes, PDFs, or images.  

- **Weakness Detection**  
  Track which concepts you struggle with most and receive recommended review material.  

- **Customizable Quizzes & Tests**  
  Generate quizzes (default 6 questions) or custom tests with your chosen number of questions. Includes **True/False** sections and a ‚ÄúI was actually right‚Äù button to mark answers correct despite minor spelling mismatches.  

- **Dark Mode**  
  Fully optimized dark interface where **everything**‚Äîflashcards, background, selections, and hover states‚Äîis dark for a comfortable study experience.  

- **Fully In-Browser & Offline Friendly**  
  All features work without an account. Your study sessions, progress, and generated content are stored locally in your browser.

## How It Works ‚ö°

1. Upload your **notes** (PDF, text, or image).  
2. AI analyzes your content to create **flashcards and quizzes**.  
3. Choose your **study mode** and begin learning.  
4. Track progress, review weak areas, and generate new quizzes or tests anytime.
